❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Installation & Setup

Q: How long does installation take?
A: Usually 1-2 minutes on a typical computer.

Q: Do I need internet to use GBS?
A: No! GBS works completely offline. All data is stored locally on your computer.

Features & Usage

Q: Can I use custom units?
A: Yes! In v3.0, the unit field is a text box. Type "Per Pair", "Bundle", or any custom unit you need.

Q: Can multiple people use GBS?
A: Yes! Create multiple user accounts with different roles (Admin, Manager, User).

Data & Backup

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes! All data stays on your computer. Automatic daily backups are created.

Q: How do I backup my data?
A: GBS automatically creates daily backups. You can also manually backup anytime from Settings.

Trial & Licensing

Q: How long is the trial?
A: 30 days from first launch with all features included.

Q: What are the trial limits?
A: 100 invoices/quotations, 3 user accounts, and community support only.
